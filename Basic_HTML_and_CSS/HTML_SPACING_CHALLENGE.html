Halloween Costume Idea : FALL

Dress up in brown
Wear leaves on your shirt.
The idea is to look like the season of Fall.
When people ask what your costume is, you fall down and say...
FALL!!

Halloween Costume Idea : "Blacked Out"

Dress up in all BLACK
When people ask what your costume is, you start to..
Stumble around
Slur your words
And say, "It's 10:00  p.m. and I'm blacked out!"
